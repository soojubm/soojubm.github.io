Editing checklist

  • Minimize pill length to two- to three-words.
  • Separate property-value pairs with a colon (“:”) and bold neither the property nor value.
  • Display dialog boxes centered horizontally and vertically over content. Always use a dark overlay to provide sufficient contrast between the box and page behind it. Never display dialogs as inline block elements. Never use a reverse color scheme for dialog boxes (ie. light text on a dark background). Always display a primary action on the right most side, with less prominent actions to its left. Always display cancel on the far left, visually separated from positive actions
